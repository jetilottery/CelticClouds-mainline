Celtic Clouds

Soar through the skies of the Emerald Isle as you search for gold in the Celtic Clouds! Re-veal 3 or more lucky gold coins behind the clouds and win prizes! The more coins you re-veal, the higher the prize. And if you’re really lucky, you’ll find the Pot of Gold and win a prize instantly!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Press each Cloud to reveal a Clover symbol, a Gold Coin symbol, or an instant win Pot of Gold symbol.

Reveal three or more Gold Coin symbols to win a prize. Revealing more Gold Coin symbols increases the prize amount.

Reveal a Pot of Gold symbol to win a prize instantly.

The game ends when all symbols are revealed. A closing message appears, indicating the game’s outcome.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to play the game to completion automatically. Game play continues until all selections have been made.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Instant wins are added to total winning prize values.

Wins display as currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
